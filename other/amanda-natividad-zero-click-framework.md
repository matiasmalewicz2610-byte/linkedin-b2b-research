## Core concept

Zero Click Content = creating content that delivers complete, standalone value within the platform itself — without requiring the reader to click through to an external website.

The insight: in an era of algorithmic feeds, platforms penalize content that tries to pull users away. LinkedIn specifically suppresses posts with external links. Zero Click Content works with the algorithm, not against it.

---

## The framework principles

1. **Lead with the insight, not the tease**
2. **Complete value in the post**
3. **Impression-first metrics**

---

## Why it works for LinkedIn B2B SaaS specifically

- LinkedIn's algorithm actively depresses reach on posts with external links (confirmed by van der Blom data)
- B2B buyers make decisions based on trust accumulated over time, not one-click conversions
- "Dark social" sharing (Chris Walker) happens when content is good enough to screenshot and share in Slack/Teams
- Zero Click posts are more likely to be saved, which is a high-weight signal for LinkedIn's algorithm

---

## Application to LinkedIn post formats

| Format | Zero Click application | Example |
|--------|----------------------|---------|
| Text post | Full framework or insight in the post | "Here are the 5 positioning mistakes that kill B2B deals: 1. [list them all]" |
| Carousel | Complete how-to guide, no CTA needed | Step-by-step process across 8-10 slides |
| Poll | Question that surfaces insight regardless of response | "What's your biggest LinkedIn content challenge?" |
| Video | Full tutorial, no "link in bio" | Screen recording of actual process |
